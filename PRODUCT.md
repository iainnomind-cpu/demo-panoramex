# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Sales agents managing tour bookings and prospects. They need quick access to customer info, communications, and booking tools in a fast-paced environment.

## Product Purpose
To streamline the booking process and increase conversion rates for Panoramex tours, providing agents with a unified view of prospects and their interactions.

## Brand Personality
Modern, sleek, premium.

## Anti-references
Not a generic Bootstrap/Material template. It should feel bespoke and tailored to the Panoramex brand, avoiding cookie-cutter administrative UI tropes.

## Design Principles
- **Speed is a feature:** Optimize workflows for agents so they can update statuses and book tours with minimal friction.
- **Data clarity over density:** Present information cleanly, ensuring key metrics and prospect details are instantly legible without visual clutter.
- **Premium aesthetic:** The interface should feel high-quality and polished, reflecting the value of the tours being sold.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Standard best practices (good contrast, keyboard navigation).
